Delicious, Juicy Baked Chicken Breast

This simple baked chicken breast recipe will yield the juiciest, flavorful, most delicious chicken breasts. The BEST part is that this recipe is SO simple to make!

You'll just need some chicken breasts and a few ingredients to make the seasoning. For this recipe, the seasoning can be made more to your specific preference. For that reason, there are no measurements for the spices. I recommend you start with a small amount and taste until it taste just right to you.

Seasoning Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1.5 tablespoons butter, sliced into smaller pieces
  • Olive oil (feel free to substitute avocado oil)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika (or feel free to just add to chicken as you go!)
  3. Coat a 9x13in pan (or larger) with olive oil
  4. Place Chicken breasts in pan and evenly coat with seasoning blend
  5. Place lemon slices in pan around the chicken
  6. Add pieces of butter on top of each chicken breast
  7. Cook about 20-25 minutes, or to 165 degrees Fahrenheit
  8. Allow chicken to rest, uncovered, for 5 minutes
  9. Slice and enjoy!
